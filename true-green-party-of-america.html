TRUE GREEN PARTY OF AMERICA

EMBRACE THE FUTURE

Welcome to the official platform of the True Green Party of America. We are dedicated to creating a sustainable and equitable future through innovative policies and bold action. Explore our comprehensive platform below and join us in making a real difference.

Energy Policy

  • Pro-Solar and Thorium Nuclear energy initiatives.
  • Massive funding for cold-fusion and quantum technology research utilizing breakthroughs of the past 10 years.
  • Anti-wind, anti-coal, anti-natural gas, anti-oil.

Federal Marriage Reform

  • All marriages of any kind recognized federally.
  • No benefits unless cohabitating, co-raising, or co-pooling finances.
  • Polyamorous marriages recognized with genuine marriage conditions.

Transgender Rights

  • Legalize informed consent and DIY practices.
  • Coverage for medications and surgeries.
  • Hormone testing for women's sports; transwomen allowed if within normal testosterone range.
  • Laws against bathroom usage deemed unconstitutional.

Housing and Property

  • Zoning law reform to promote multifamily dwellings.
  • Ban foreigners from owning property unless they are residents.
  • Ban citizens from owning more than two residential properties.

End Corporate Personhood

  • End the recognition of corporations as people.
  • Make lobbying illegal.

Universal Healthcare

  • Federal healthcare for all, including international students, temporary workers, and residents.
  • Includes dental and vision coverage.
  • Pharma companies incentivized to focus on cures, not temporary treatments.
  • Eliminate private health insurance.

Infrastructure and Services

  • Nationalize trains, electricity, water, and internet infrastructure.
  • Develop a national high-speed rail network.
  • Provide free public transit.

Trade Policies

  • Completely embargo Israel.
  • Raise tariffs on China to make Chinese goods uncompetitive.
  • End free trade where it doesn't benefit the U.S. working class.

Economic Reforms

  • Implement a universal basic income guaranteeing food and housing for all citizens and residents.
  • Make homelessness illegal with support systems in place.
  • Create 'Favela Land' for anarchistic living without tax support.
  • Minimum wage set to automatically increase with inflation.
  • End tipping culture.

Education and Social Services

  • Provide free university education up to PhD level.
  • Offer free daycare and adult retraining programs.
  • Cancel federal and private student debt.
  • Mandatory paid parental leave (4 years), split as couples see fit.
  • Universal elder care.
  • Guarantee a minimum of 6 weeks vacation annually.

Environmental Policies

  • Ban plastic use in food packaging.
  • Enforce right-to-repair laws.

Electoral Reforms

  • Implement ranked-choice preferential voting.
  • Enforce only public financing of elections.
  • Make election day a holiday with automatic voter registration.
  • Abolish the Senate.
  • Supreme Court reform.

Media Integrity

  • Reestablish journalistic integrity; media cannot claim "entertainment" to avoid accountability.
  • Strengthen FOIA regulations.

Statehood for All

  • Grant statehood to all American dependencies.

Economic Accountability

  • Nationalize the Federal Reserve.
  • Hold everyone responsible for illegal acts to life imprisonment.

Privacy and Surveillance

  • Abolish the CIA, NSA, ATF, and other subversive agencies.
  • Gut the FBI and DOJ; remake them if possible.
  • Establish a federal work program for domestic and foreign projects.

Join us in building a sustainable and just future!
